Instructions

  1. Click the bird and pull back to anywhere between the yellow indicted area to launch the bird. You can change the angle and magnitude of the birds initial launch depending on your pull.
  2. Defeat all piggies in each level to proceed to the next level.
  3. All Objects that are hit will increase your score before sure to hit blocks as well as pigs to beat the highscore!
